History DPS Faridabad was founded by Shri U.S. Verma in 1995 at Sector 19, Faridabad.[2][3] Infrastructure DPS Faridabad covers an area of 8 acres (32,000 m2) and includes a hostel, a playing field, a canteen, an open-air theatre, gardens, and a school building.[citation needed] The School has a SIS (student information system) Department for effective digital information and communication. The school has installed a solar power plant on its rooftop to meet all of its power requirements and even supplies electricity to the Haryana Government. The school currently enrolls over 4000 students as day scholars and hostelers and conducts classes from preschool to senior secondary level.[4] It also conducts a social responsibility programme called DPS Shiksha Kendra for underprivileged children, providing them with equipment such as books, stationery and uniforms as well as education. It is managed by Delhi Public School Society.[5] The school has tie-up with Bangalore-based company for their app Northstar to ensure child safety by tracking their movement to and from school.[6] All transports are highly equipped with CCTV cameras for surveillance. Sports 1) DPS Faridabad participates in regional athletics competitions. 2) Vikrant Panchal is an Indian athlete, also studied in DPS Faridabad. 3) Somansh Singh is a National Cricketer who represents Haryana. 4) Rahul Dalal is Indian Domestic Cricketer who played for Haryana and is currently playing for Arunachal CA, studied in DPS Faridabad. 5) Rhythm Sangwan is an Indian shooter who won the Gold medal at the Junior World Championship held in Lima, Peru (2021).[7]
